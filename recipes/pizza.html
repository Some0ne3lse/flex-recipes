Pizza!

Homemade Vegetarian Pizza

Is there anything better than pizza? Yes there is. Homemade pizza.

In this article I will describe how to make my usual homemade pizza. Grab a shopping bag and run to the store, because this pizza is delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pizza Cheese
  • Onion
  • Cocktail Tomatoes (Normal Tomatoes work as well)
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pesto
  • Optional: Basil

Steps

  1. Start by pre-heating the oven to 200Â°C
  2. Spread the dough out with a rolling pin. If you don't want to deal with it getting stuck on the counter, my solution is to do it straight on the baking paper.
  3. Spread the Pizza Sauce over the pizza.
  4. Spread the cheese over the pizza sauce. Try not to eat all of the cheese in advance. I sometimes fail at this.
  5. Cut the Onion in small circles. Spread them over the pizza.
  6. Chop the Cocktail Tomatoes in half. If you're using normal Tomatoes, try cutting them in wedges. Spread them over the pizza.
  7. Spread the cream cheese in small piles over the pizza.
  8. Finally, add the pesto and basil and put it in the oven!
  9. Let it sit in the oven for 8 - 12 minutes.
  10. When finished, let it cool down for a few minutes. Otherwise you might do what I've done plenty of times and burn your mouth.
