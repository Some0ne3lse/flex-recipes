Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Now, this one is one I don't make often anymore, but there was a time where the majority of my meals consisted of this recipe.

If you have the money, and somewhere to buy it, fresh Basil makes all the difference here. I know, dry Basil is cheaper and easier, and most of the time when I buy it fresh, I end up throwing half of it out because it spoils too fast. But this dish is ten times better with fresh Basil.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Any vegetable that you find suitable (Like carrots, broccoli etc.)
  • Salt
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Start by frying chopped Onions in Oil in a pan.
  2. Add Tomato Paste, Garlic and Salt.
  3. After a few minutes add chopped Vegetables.
  4. When the Vegetables are soft, add chopped tomatoes and a bit of freshly cut Basil.
  5. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. While the Tomato Sauce simmers, boil your Spaghetti in water with a pinch of salt.
  7. Add a little more Basil to the Tomato Sauce.
  8. When the Spaghetti is ready, remove the water from the sauce pan.
  9. Optional: Add butter to the Spaghetti.
  10. Remove the Tomato Sauce from heat.
  11. Combine Spaghetti and Tomato Sauce and eat!
